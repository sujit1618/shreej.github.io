Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 March 2026  ·  Shree J Printers Private Limited

This Privacy Policy describes how Shree J Printers Private Limited collects, uses, and safeguards personal information shared through our official WhatsApp Business channel. By initiating or continuing a conversation with us on WhatsApp, you agree to the practices described herein.
01

About Us

Shree J Printers Private Limited is a premier commercial printing company based in Pune, Maharashtra, India, specialising in high-volume offset printing, UV printing, custom packaging, and book production — serving B2B clients nationally and internationally since 2003.

Our WhatsApp Business account is operated solely for communicating with clients, vendors, and partners regarding printing orders, job updates, delivery schedules, quotations, and related business matters.

02

Information We Collect

When you contact us via WhatsApp, we may collect and retain the following:

  • Your name and WhatsApp phone number
  • Messages, images, documents, and files you share with us
  • Job details, order specifications, and instructions you provide
  • Timestamps and delivery status of messages
  • Any other information you voluntarily share during the conversation

We do not collect information without your direct engagement, and we do not use automated scraping or tracking methods on WhatsApp.

03

How We Use Your Information

Information shared through WhatsApp is used exclusively for:

  • Processing and managing print job orders and quotations
  • Communicating job status, proofs, and delivery updates
  • Responding to your queries, complaints, or feedback
  • Maintaining records of business transactions as required by law
  • Improving the quality and efficiency of our services

We will not use your information for unsolicited marketing or share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

04

Sharing of Information

Shree J Printers Private Limited does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Internal staff: Only employees directly involved in fulfilling your order will access relevant conversation details
  • Legal compliance: If required by applicable Indian law, court order, or government authority
  • Service partners: Third-party logistics or delivery vendors who require order details solely to complete your delivery — bound by confidentiality obligations
05

WhatsApp Platform & Meta's Policies

Our WhatsApp Business account operates on the platform provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. By using WhatsApp to communicate with us, your data is also subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy and Meta's Terms of Service.

We encourage you to review WhatsApp's privacy practices at whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy. Shree J Printers Private Limited is not responsible for data processing conducted by Meta or WhatsApp on their own platforms.

06

Data Retention

We retain WhatsApp conversation records for as long as reasonably necessary — typically for the duration of an active business relationship and up to three (3) years thereafter for accounting and legal record-keeping purposes.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Requests will be honoured subject to any legal retention obligations.

07

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect information shared with us. WhatsApp messages are end-to-end encrypted by default on the WhatsApp platform. Access to our WhatsApp Business account is restricted to authorised personnel only.

However, no method of electronic communication is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over WhatsApp or the internet.

08

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations
  • Withdraw consent to receive communications from us at any time by sending "STOP" or notifying us directly
09

Children's Privacy

Our WhatsApp Business channel is intended for business clients, vendors, and adults only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has shared information with us, please contact us immediately.

10

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. The most current version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of our WhatsApp Business channel after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11
